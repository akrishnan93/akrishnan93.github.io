Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 21, 2026

Overview

Sports Imposter ("the App") is a party game where players try to identify the imposter who doesn't know the secret sports player. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how the App works.

Information We Collect

The App itself does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information. However, the App uses the following third-party services:

Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)

  • The App displays rewarded interstitial ads to free users.
  • AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and ad interaction data to serve and measure ads.
  • For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Firebase Analytics

  • We use Firebase Analytics to understand how the App is used (e.g., which screens are viewed, which sports are popular).
  • Firebase may collect device identifiers, app usage data, and general location (country/region).
  • No personally identifiable information is collected.
  • For more information, see Firebase Privacy Information.

Apple In-App Purchases

  • Subscriptions and purchases are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store.
  • We do not collect or store any payment information.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Precise location data
  • Photos, files, or other personal content
  • Health, financial, or sensitive personal data

Ad-Free Experience

Users who subscribe to Sports Imposter PRO will not see any ads. When ads are removed, AdMob does not collect data from those users.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App's ad implementation follows Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework.

Data Storage

Game preferences (selected sports, difficulty settings) are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date above.
